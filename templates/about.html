About App

This app was made for the Udacity FullStack Web Development NanoDegree.

This is a Movie Trailer application that stores my favorite movies.
Upon opening the application all you have to do is click on any given movie poster and the trailer will play, via an embedded youtube iframe element.
This application is made with multliple technologies, here are the main ones I had the pleasure working with Python, Flask framework, sqlalchemy, sqlite.
In the credits link within the navbar you will find where I credit the resources I used to complete this app.
